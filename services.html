Logic Games Tutoring

The Logic Games section (officially called Analytical Reasoning) is often the most challenging for LSAT takers. My tutoring approach helps you master the fundamental skills needed to excel in this section:

  • Diagram creation techniques for all game types
  • Rule interpretation and application
  • Inference recognition and deduction
  • Time management strategies
  • Pattern recognition across different game types

Through targeted practice and personalized feedback, you'll develop the analytical skills needed to tackle even the most complex logic games with confidence.

Logical Reasoning Tutoring

The Logical Reasoning section makes up half of your LSAT score, making it crucial for success. My tutoring focuses on:

  • Argument structure analysis
  • Identifying premises and conclusions
  • Recognizing common logical fallacies
  • Strengthening and weakening arguments
  • Parallel reasoning and conditional logic
  • Question type-specific strategies

I'll help you develop a systematic approach to each question type, enabling you to quickly identify what's being asked and how to approach it efficiently.

Reading Comprehension Tutoring

The Reading Comprehension section tests your ability to understand complex texts and answer detailed questions. My tutoring approach includes:

  • Active reading techniques
  • Passage mapping and annotation
  • Main point and purpose identification
  • Author's attitude and tone recognition
  • Comparative passage strategies
  • Speed reading without sacrificing comprehension

I'll help you develop efficient reading strategies that allow you to extract key information quickly while maintaining accuracy.
